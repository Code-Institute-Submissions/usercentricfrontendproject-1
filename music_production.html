The full course curriculum:

Module 1. Overview:

  • Principles of producing music (designing, composing, arranging, mixing and mastering)
  • Getting ready/ getting started

Module 2. Design:

  • Intro to synthesis: types of oscillator (analog, wavetable, fm, multi, noise)
  • Synthesis - pads and textures
  • Synthesis- lead sounds (including arps)
  • Synthesis - bass
  • Drums - drum machines
  • Drums - sampling
  • Sampling - real life instruments
  • Sampling - abstract

Module 3. Effects:

  • Distortion and overdrive
  • Modulation (chorus, phaser, flanger)
  • reverb
  • Delay/echo
  • Filters

Module 4. Composing:

  • Creating the backbone of a track
  • Getting out of 'loopitis'
  • Basics of time signatures/beats/bars
  • Recording and playing 'live'
  • Using the pencil tool
  • Quantization

Module 5. Arranging:

  • Creating movement in sounds (using automation, envelopes and modulation)
  • How elements work together
  • Building/decreasing energy. basic song structure

Module 6. Mixing:

  • Sound in space - 3D model of width, depth and height explained
  • Width - Stereo field - Panning
  • Depth - Front/back placement of sounds /loud and bright vs dull and quiet - Dynamics
  • Height - Managing frequencies (balance of instruments and frequencies)

Module 7. Mastering:

  • Equalising (EQ) - Fine tuning frequencies
  • Compression
  • Limiting
  • Finishing touches

Bonus module:

  • Principles of practice/ learning/ improving
  • 10 general do's and donts to remember
  • Software specific tips, tricks and shortcuts
  • Guide to building a home studio
  • Using social media and marketing to find more exposure for your music
