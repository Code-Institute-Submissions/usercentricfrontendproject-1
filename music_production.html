Do you have a song in your head just waiting to be fully realised?

They say that 'everyone has one good book in them'. Well, I believe the same is true about music or even individual sounds. The human imagination is so wildly creative that for many people, if their creativity is not channelled in the right way, they can feel uncomfortable or act out of character. What if I told you that you are potentially weeks away from learning a new language, so to speak, that could give you a voice and a form of expression unlike anything you have experienced before?

This is not some 'rock star' course, I cannot guarantee you any adoring fans or even mild success. But I can teach you everything I have learned in the decade-plus that I have been creating music and sounds. Using what I have learned through trial and error over many years, I have created a complete roadmap to become a competent music producer. The creativity is down to the indivual, nobody can teach you how to write a good song. But I can arm you with all the tools you need to create the music that you want, from making the indivual sounds, to arranging them together, to fine-tuning every little element until you have maximised the potential of your idea, from a tiny seed in your imagination to a fully fledged song that you can share with the world (if thats what you desire), or just to give yourself some musical therapy, basking in the sounds that you created yourself.
