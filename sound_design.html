Making sounds from scratch is great fun! It's so easy to lose hours in the nitty-gritty of tweaking sounds and experimenting with different methods.

But sometimes, if you need to keep the direction of your track without getting lost in individual elements, it helps to have your desired sounds just a click away so that you can focus on the song, not the components. As someone with over ten years experience, I know as much as anyone that having a library of presets can shave so much time in the process of making a song from beginning to end. I offer a bespoke sound design service for those who want to maximise their time bringing the music in their head to life - I will put the time in to explore and craft the sounds, so that you can concentrate on the bigger picture and realise your musical dreams!

I can cater for any tastes, and you can specify exactly what you want per mood, instrument, style, or any other metric you can think of. whether you need spacey synths, dominant drums or bouncy basses, I can craft the sounds that you visualise, so that your song can be realised!
